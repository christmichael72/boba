About Us

BOBALICIOUS is a bubble tea brand originated in Indonesia and we are established in 2020. Our intention is to serve the freshest boba drinks to the world, to use our creativity to develop more new boba flavors, and to let the world taste the best flavor of Taiwan boba pearl milk tea through taste and vision.
